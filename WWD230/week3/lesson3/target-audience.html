Target Audience: Target audience for this websites will be adults who are interested in the weather activity of the area so they can plan their activies. It will have forcasted weather, and show diagrams of weather patterns in the area. Adam loves gardening, but needs a weather site to check what plants to start on now that can handle colder weather, and what protection he needs for them.
Melanie loves to go on walks, but always dresses for the wrong weather.

Scenarios: Jill is a mother of three kids, and they want to go on a camping trip for the weekend, but she wants to check the weather in the area they want to camping in so they can decide if this weekend is the best weekend to go or if they shouldl wait till next week. She uses the website to look at the weather for the weekend and sees that there will be a slight breeze, but it should be good enough weather to go camping.

Adam is deciding when the best time to plant his garden is, and what kind of protection he should put over them. He checks the Kotter Weather to see what the weather will be like for the next week and decides that a cover for his plants overnight would help them stay alive until better weather comes, but it should be warm enough to start planting.

Melanie wants to go on a hike in a week up the mountain, but doesn't know if it'll be good enough weather to, and if she should dress for hot weather or warm weather with a breeze. She checks out Kotter Weather and sees it'll be really hot this weekend, so she should dress light and bring lots of water.

Jeri and John want to go on a trip to Utah this weekend, but they've heard that there is going to be a blizzard this week. They check on Kotter Weather and see that there is a forcasted blizzard come, and decide to delay their trip till traveling weather is better.

Taylor is deciding whether it's worth it to mow the lawn yet, because the weather has been finicky in idaho. She hops onto Kotter Weather and sees that it's supposed to snow twice this next week. She decides to wait to mow till the snow goes away.
